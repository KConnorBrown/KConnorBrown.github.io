Mondrian Grid
Javascript, (deprecated JavaFX)

Implemented a recursive algorithm that subdivides a canvas into a rectilinear grid. Users create artwork in the distinct style of Piet Mondrian by filling in regions with yellow, red, and blue. Users can save and share their work as a png.
